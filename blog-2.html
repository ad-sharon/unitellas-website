STRENGHTENING YOUR CUSTOMER SLAs WITH zStorage QUALIITY OF SERVICES

Published on 29 April 2022 | Sharon


Let’s face it, the 2020 pandemic made it clear to even those who aren’t in IT, that those not paying attention to the fact that the demands and requirements of service providers and enterprise IT organisations is only getting more demanding. With more applications being deployed faster than one would expect, and higher demand for agile resources to operate Enterprise IT organizations efficiently. Companies are in need of on-demand infrastructure solutions with the capacity to deliver predictably and empower them to operate better, innovate more quickly, and respond to applications' demands and business challenges faster than users can keep up with.


At the heart of our Infrastructure-as-a-Service is our multi-tenancy which enables multiple customers or applications to use the same infrastructure. Don’t worry, we are quite aware of the “noisy neighbors” or to be more precise, the more performance-sensitive applications which when consolidated to a single platform causes unpredictable performances and result in not so happy customers. So, how did we fix this?


zStorage Virtual Private Storage Arrays(VPSA): A Multi-Tenant Storage Architecture

We achieve the concept of multi-tenancy by merging the architecture of Cloud Computing with Block, File, and Object storage into a highly scalable and dependable system which allows the tenants control their storage while allowing you the Enterprise IT organization to allocate on-demand resources such as CPU, network and storage drives to each of your customers eliminating the “noisy neighbor” issue. As a result, each customer has a private environment, access to their performance data for monitoring, and a management console for control and tuning of the environment. This allows enterprise IT organizations to allocate different amount of resources to different users depending on their needs and requirements.

Well, if you're wondering what that is, it’s simply building on our VPSA architecture to empower our enterprise IT organisation partners to serve their customers with additional means of control and delivery of their SLAs. The ability to set performance thresholds on their zStorage Virtual Private Arrays (VPSA) based storage volumes, which allows for appropriate allocation of performance, so that the system delivers consistently on all workloads. This also allows for unprecedented control over performance of large-scale multi-tenant environments. By setting the QoS levels on multi-tenant environments, customers get to solidify their performance SLAs.
