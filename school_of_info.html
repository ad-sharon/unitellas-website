What is the Unitellas School of Information?

The objectives and purpose of the Unitellas School of Information are as follows:

To provide cost-effective career and professional education in Nigeria, Africa, and beyond to bridge the gap in the Business and Technology Eco-System of Nigeria and Africa.

To engage in innovative research, development, and technology commercialization, to contribute to national and regional development.

To promote academic excellence, social responsibility, and cultural exchange through high-quality training, experiential learning, and community engagement.

At Unitellas International Limited, we empower African Telco's and Cloud Service Providers by providing on-premise compute, network and storage as a service while supporting SME's and large Enterprises to eliminate infrastructure & financial risks with our various service models.
