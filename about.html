About Us

GET FULLY-MANAGED, PAY-FOR-USE CLOUD SERVICES ON-PREMISES, HYBRID, MULTI-CLOUD, OR AT THE EDGE.

Unitellas International Limited is a leading IT company registered in Nigeria that deals with cloud services. We are the official distributor for Zadara Cloud in Africa, delivering edge cloud infrastructure as a service to telecommunications operators and cloud service providers.

We empower african teleco's and cloud service providers by providing access to on-demand, enterprise-grade compute, networking and storage as a service (IaaS), designed to lower your costs, future-proof your infrastructure, and handle any workload.

We provide storage, compute, and networking services with a ZERO CapEx.

Pay-As-You-Go pricing

Zero egress fees

Fully managed 24/7/365

VMs and storage centers worldwide

On-premise, hybrid or cloud

AWS Compatible APIs
